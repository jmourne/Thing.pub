### In-App Analytics
To help the Author refine the work, this platform utilizes anonymized reader analytics. Our goal is to understand the "rhythm" of the story, not to monitor the individual.
- What We Track: We measure "Dwell Time" (time spent per page), "Reader Fall-off" (where engagement stops), and "Continuation Rates" (how quickly a reader moves to the next chapter).
- Anonymization: This data is aggregated to show trends (e.g., "60% of readers paused at Chapter 4"). We do not use this data for marketing, nor is it sold to third parties.
- Purpose: These insights allow the Author to deduce which sections are functioning as intended and which may require refinement to meet the vision of the project.
